What this system does

ChilliTech digitizes the daily operations of a cold storage business. Farmers interact entirely through WhatsApp — in Telugu or English, by text or voice note. Operators (employees and owners) manage the business through a web dashboard, and also receive WhatsApp confirmations for quick actions.

📦 Inward

Farmer deposits produce. System registers the lot, records weight, assigns a chamber, and sends a confirmation with a lot ID.

🚚 Outward

Farmer requests withdrawal. System calculates dues, operator confirms, lot is released, and a receipt is sent.

📋 Receipts

A structured WhatsApp confirmation is sent after every inward and outward. Full PDF receipts in a later phase.

💰 Ledger

Advances, dues, and payments are tracked per farmer. All entries are append-only — nothing is ever silently erased.

The golden rule

The AI proposes. A human confirms. No inward, outward, or financial action happens automatically. The AI reads the farmer's message and creates a pending action. An operator must confirm it before anything changes in the system. This is non-negotiable — a wrong release means real chilli leaving the gate wrongly.

Roles at a Glance

Three roles, one hierarchy. Each inherits everything from the role below it.

Inheritance: Owner ⊃ Employee ⊃ Farmer. An owner can do everything an employee can do, and an employee can do everything a farmer can do — plus more.

🌾 Farmer WhatsApp only

Interacts entirely via WhatsApp. No login, no app. Authenticated by their phone number.

  • ✅ Ask about their own lot status ("Where is my lot?")
  • ✅ Check their own balance / advance taken
  • ✅ Request inward (deposit) — AI proposes, operator confirms
  • ✅ Request outward (withdrawal) — AI proposes, operator confirms
  • ✅ Receive WhatsApp confirmation/receipt after actions
  • ❌ See other farmers' data
  • ❌ Directly execute any action — all go through operator confirmation
  • ❌ Access the web dashboard

👷 Employee WhatsApp + Dashboard

Manages day-to-day operations. Has a dashboard login. Owner can restrict individual permissions.

  • ✅ Everything a farmer can do, for any farmer
  • ✅ Confirm or reject AI-proposed actions (the key daily task)
  • ✅ Initiate inward/outward on behalf of a farmer ("proxy")
  • ✅ View farmer ledgers and balances
  • ✅ View all active lots and chambers
  • ✅ Review and resolve ambiguous/low-confidence AI messages
  • ❌ Make adjustments to weights or financial records (unless owner grants permission)
  • ❌ Manage other employees
  • ❌ Change storage charge rates or system config

👑 Owner Full access

Full control. Can grant or restrict employee permissions. Sees everything.

  • ✅ Everything an employee can do
  • ✅ Make adjustments to records (with a logged reason)
  • ✅ Manage employees — add, deactivate, set permissions
  • ✅ View the full audit trail
  • ✅ View all financial records across all farmers
  • ✅ Change system config (charge rates, chambers, etc.)
  • ✅ Add new farmer consent and manage farmer data

Farmer sends a WhatsApp message requesting to deposit produce. AI parses it, operator confirms, lot is created.

Farmer Flow — Market Price Query

Farmer asks for today's price range for their commodity — to help decide whether to hold or release their stored lot.

FarmerAI Bot
Why this is in-scope (not a general market query): The farmer is asking about the price of produce currently sitting in this cold storage. Helping them decide whether to hold or release is a direct part of the cold storage business relationship. This is different from asking "what's the weather" — it drives a potential outward action.

How price data works

📝 Employee enters price each morning

The employee (or owner) enters today's price range per commodity in the dashboard. Takes about 2 minutes. Historical values are stored permanently for future seasonality analysis.

📅 Fallback: latest available price

If today's price hasn't been entered yet (or it's a public holiday), the bot shows the most recent available price with an "as of [date]" label — never shows stale data silently.

📈 History stored from day one

Every price entry is kept with its date. This builds a price history over time — useful for the operator to spot seasonal patterns (Phase 2 analytics).

Farmer Flow — Release Request (Full or Partial)

Farmer signals intent to release all or part of their stored lot. An employee calls to align on quantity, price, and date — then processes the release as a proxy action.

FarmerAI BotEmployee
Why the phone call step? Release decisions — especially partial ones — involve agreeing on exact quantity, timing, buyer, and sometimes price. This can't be safely resolved via WhatsApp message alone. The operator calls the farmer to align, then enters the agreed details as a proxy release. This keeps the process accurate and the operator informed.

Step-by-step sequence

1
Farmer signals release intent via WhatsApp
"I want to release my lot" / "I want to sell some of my chilli" / "Release 100 bags from GNT1-20260606-0001"
2
Bot acknowledges and shows current balance
Shows lot details, dues so far, net balance. Tells farmer that an operator will call them to confirm the details.
3
Release request appears in employee dashboard queue
Flagged as "needs phone call" — not a standard confirm/reject. Employee sees farmer's message and contact number.
4
Employee calls the farmer
They agree on: full or partial release, exact quantity (if partial), date, any price considerations. Employee notes the agreed details.
5
Employee enters the release in dashboard (proxy action)
Enters agreed quantity, date, notes. Creates a pending action on behalf of the farmer. Owner or employee confirms.
✅ Audit trail: "Employee Suresh on behalf of Farmer Raju Kumar — agreed via phone call on 06 Jun 2026"
6
Farmer receives WhatsApp confirmation
Release confirmed message with lot ID, quantity released, dues, and net payable. Same message whether full or partial.

In-scope vs Out-of-scope Messages

The bot handles cold-storage operations only. Anything outside that boundary is redirected — never answered as a general assistant.

FarmerAI Bot
WhatsApp policy (RULE 8): Meta bans general-purpose AI chatbots on the WhatsApp Business API. Structured business-task bots are allowed. The boundary must be clear — anything outside cold-storage operations is redirected, not answered.

Employee Flow — Confirm or Reject AI Actions

The most important daily task. All AI-proposed actions sit in a queue until an operator acts on them.

EmployeeAI Bot
This is the heart of the system. Every time a farmer sends an inward or outward request, it lands here as a "pending action." The employee reviews the details, checks for errors, and either confirms or rejects. Nothing moves without this step.

Two ways to confirm — WhatsApp or Dashboard

📱 Via WhatsApp (quick)

For simple, clear actions (high confidence), the employee gets a WhatsApp notification. They reply YES or NO. Fastest path for routine inwards.

🖥 Via Dashboard (full review)

For anything complex — partial withdrawals, financial adjustments, ambiguous messages, or low-confidence parses — the employee goes to the dashboard queue.

Employee Flow — Low-Confidence Review Queue

When the AI is unsure what a farmer meant, the message goes to a human review queue instead of creating a pending action.

AI BotEmployee
Why this matters: Voice notes in rural Telugu with crop terminology often have ambiguity. If confidence is below the threshold, the system never guesses — it sends the message to a human. "Low confidence → review" is a hard rule.
1
Farmer sends an ambiguous voice note
e.g. a noisy recording, mixed Telugu/English, incomplete information ("take out some bags").
2
AI transcribes and parses — low confidence
AI can't reliably extract all entities. Confidence score below threshold.
3
Bot tells farmer a human will follow up
"I didn't quite understand. Our team will contact you shortly to confirm the details."
4
Message appears in employee review queue
Employee sees the original message (voice/text), the AI's best-guess parse, and the confidence score. They can manually create the correct action.
5
Employee contacts farmer (WA or phone), enters correct details
Proceeds as a manual proxy action. Full audit trail preserved.

Security assessment — how the risk label is calculated

Formula (computed live, never stored):

security_value = SUM(net_weight of pledged active lots)
                × current_market_price (latest employee entry for that commodity)
                × security_factor (e.g. 0.75 — configured per tenant)

Secured           → security_value ≥ outstanding_balance
Partially secured → 0 < security_value < outstanding_balance
Unsecured         → no active pledged stock

The security factor (haircut) accounts for price volatility and selling costs. It is a per-tenant config value — the owner can adjust it. Changing it immediately re-labels all loans in the portfolio view.

Farmer — Help Card

What farmers can do, and how. Use this to explain the system to a new farmer.

🌾 As a Farmer, you can:

  • ✅ Check your lot status — message "lot status" or "where is my chilli"
  • ✅ Check your balance — message "my balance" or "how much advance"
  • ✅ Request inward (deposit) — message "I want to deposit 100 bags Teja"
  • ✅ Request outward (withdrawal) — message "I want to release lot [lot ID]"
  • ✅ Get a receipt — sent automatically after every confirmed action
  • ❌ You cannot release your own lot — an operator must always confirm
  • ❌ You cannot see other farmers' information

How to reach the system

WhatsApp only. Send a message to the business WhatsApp number. You can write in English or Telugu, or send a voice note. The system will reply within seconds.

If the system doesn't understand: An operator will contact you directly. You can also call the office at any time.

Your lot ID — keep it safe

Every time you deposit produce, you get a Lot ID (e.g. GNT1-20260606-0001). This is your receipt number. Keep this ID — you'll need it when requesting a withdrawal or asking about that deposit.

WhatsApp messages you can send (examples)

You can say…System will…
"My lot status" / "Where is my chilli?"Show your active lots with quantity, chamber, dues today, and net balance
"My balance" / "How much advance?"Show the same snapshot — advance + dues + net payable to you
"Price Teja" / "Teja price today?"Show today's Teja price range (or latest available with date if not yet updated)
"334 price" / "Guntur Sannam rate?"Show that variety's price range
"I want to deposit 100 bags of Teja"Note the request — operator confirms and assigns chamber
"I want to release my lot" / "I want to sell"Acknowledge and show your balance — operator will call you to agree on details
"I want to sell 300 bags from my lot"Same as above — operator calls to confirm the partial release details
"How much storage charge for my lot?"Show dues accrued so far on your active lot(s)
For release requests: the bot acknowledges your message and an operator will call you. Please keep your phone available. The release only happens after you and the operator agree on the details — nothing leaves the storage without that conversation.

Employee — Help Card

What employees do daily, how to handle each situation. For the India-based operator.

👷 As an Employee, your daily tasks are:

  • ✅ Check the pending queue every morning — confirm or reject AI-proposed actions
  • ✅ Reply YES/NO on WhatsApp for simple inward/outward notifications
  • ✅ Handle the review queue — voice notes or messages the AI couldn't parse clearly
  • ✅ Register inwards directly (proxy) when a farmer arrives in person or calls
  • ✅ Look up farmer balances and lot details for any farmer
  • ❌ You cannot change a confirmed record — only the owner can make adjustments (or grant you this permission)
  • ❌ You cannot change storage charge rates or system config

Morning routine

1
Open dashboard → Pending queue
Check if any overnight WhatsApp messages created pending actions.
2
Confirm, reject, or flag each pending action
For inwards: enter weight and assign chamber. For outwards: verify dues before confirming release.
3
Handle the review queue
Contact farmers with unclear messages, enter the correct action manually.
4
Register any in-person arrivals
Use "New inward (proxy)" for farmers who arrived at the storage without messaging first.

When in doubt

When uncertain, reject and contact the farmer directly. It is always safer to reject a pending action and ask the farmer to confirm via WhatsApp again than to confirm something incorrectly. The audit trail records every rejection too.

Owner — Help Card

What owners can do beyond employees. Focus on oversight, config, and exception handling.

👑 As an Owner, you have full control:

  • ✅ Everything the employee can do
  • ✅ Adjust records — correct a wrong weight or ledger entry (logged with reason)
  • ✅ Manage staff — add employees, deactivate accounts, set permissions per person
  • ✅ View the full audit trail — see exactly who did what and when
  • ✅ Change system config — storage charge rates, chamber layout, varieties
  • ✅ See all financial records — advances, dues, settlements across all farmers
  • ✅ Delete or anonymize farmer data on request (privacy compliance)

Making an adjustment (correcting a record)

Adjustments are always logged. You cannot silently change a weight or financial record. Every adjustment requires a written reason and is permanently recorded in the audit log — who changed it, what it was before, what it is now, and why.
1
Go to the lot or ledger entry to correct
2
Click "Make adjustment" — enter new value and reason
e.g. "Weight corrected after re-weighment — original bag count was wrong"
3
Audit log entry created automatically
Old value, new value, reason, your name, and timestamp are all recorded permanently.

Glossary

Key terms used in the system — for explaining to the uncle, employees, or farmers.

TermWhat it means
LotA single deposit of produce by one farmer. Gets a unique Lot ID (e.g. GNT1-20260606-0001). One farmer can have multiple active lots.
Lot IDThe unique code for each deposit. Format: {FACILITY_CODE}-{DATE}-{NUMBER}. The farmer's receipt number.
InwardWhen produce is brought in and deposited at the cold storage.
OutwardWhen produce is taken out (withdrawn) from cold storage.
Pending actionAn action proposed by the AI (or a farmer's WhatsApp message) that is waiting for an operator to confirm or reject. Nothing happens until a human says yes.
AdvanceMoney given to the farmer by the operator at the time of deposit. Tracked in the ledger and deducted from dues at outward.
DuesStorage charges owed by the farmer — calculated as days stored × charge rate × quantity.
LedgerThe financial record for each farmer — every advance, charge, payment, and settlement. Append-only: nothing is ever erased.
Audit logA permanent record of every action in the system — who did it, when, on whose behalf, and what changed.
AdjustmentA correction to an existing record (weight, ledger entry). Always requires a written reason. Permanently logged.
Proxy actionWhen an employee or owner performs an action on behalf of a farmer (e.g. registering an inward because the farmer arrived in person).
ChamberA section of the cold storage where produce is stored. Each lot is assigned to a chamber.
TenantA customer of ChilliTech — one cold storage business. tenant_001 = uncle's business. All data is strictly separated per tenant.
Facility codeA short code for a storage location (e.g. GNT1 = Guntur facility 1). Used in Lot IDs. One tenant can have multiple facilities.
AI confidenceHow sure the AI is that it correctly understood a farmer's message. Low confidence → goes to human review queue instead of creating a pending action.
24-hour windowWhatsApp allows free messages within 24 hours of the last farmer message. Outside this, only pre-approved template messages can be sent.
Market priceThe daily price range per commodity variety, entered by the employee each morning from Guntur market rates. Farmers can query this via WhatsApp to help decide whether to hold or release their stored produce.
Price historyEvery daily price entry is stored permanently. Over time this builds a record useful for spotting seasonal price patterns (Phase 2 analytics).
Partial release / partial outwardReleasing only a portion of a stored lot. The farmer messages intent, the employee calls to agree on quantity and timing, then enters the release as a proxy action. The remaining quantity stays in storage under the same lot ID with status "Partial".
Release requestA farmer's WhatsApp signal that they want to release all or part of their stored produce. Triggers an employee phone call to align on details — not a direct action.
Running statementThe employee's view of a farmer's full history — two parallel running totals: inventory movements (kg in / kg out / balance) and financial entries (advances / charges / settlements / net payable). The authoritative view before confirming any outward.
